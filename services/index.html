Our Services

From concept to installation, we deliver a seamless experience rooted in excellence.

Custom Design

Collaborate with our designers to create a door that reflects your vision. Every detail, from scrollwork to finish, is tailored to your specifications.

Handcrafted Build

Our craftsmen bring your design to life using heavy-duty 14-gauge steel, precision welding, and premium finishes built to last.

Expert Installation

Our skilled installers ensure a perfect fit and flawless finish, from removal of your existing door to final hardware alignment.

Strength & Security

Iron doors offer exceptional durability and peace of mind. Polyurethane-filled cores and tempered, sealed glass add energy efficiency and weather resistance.

Custom Finishes & Styles

From sleek modern to ornate traditional, choose from a variety of finishes and intricate designs to match your home.

Consultation

Not sure where to start? Our complimentary design consultation helps you explore styles, sizes, and possibilities for your perfect entrance.

Why Custom Wrought Iron?

Custom wrought iron doors add security, curb appeal, and lasting value to your home. Unlike off-the-shelf options, every piece we create is designed for your opening, your style, and your vision. We handle design, fabrication, finish, and installation so you get one point of contact and one exceptional result.

Our Process: First Contact to Final Delivery

First Contact & Consultation

Reach out by phone, email, or our contact form. We'll schedule a complimentary consultation to discuss your project: style preferences, dimensions, timeline, and budget. We can meet at your property for measurements or at our Las Vegas showroom so you can see and touch our work.

Design & Quote

We create a design that fits your opening and aesthetic, and provide a clear, detailed quote. You'll know exactly what's included: materials, finish, hardware options, and installation. No hidden fees.

Approval & Deposit

Once you approve the design and quote, we secure your project with a deposit and add you to our build schedule. We'll give you an estimated completion and installation window.

Fabrication

Our craftsmen build your door to spec in our shop: cutting, welding, finishing, and quality checks. We keep you updated if anything changes and will send photos when the door is ready.

Delivery & Installation

We deliver and install your door with care. We handle removal of the old door (if needed), fit, hardware, and cleanup. You get a walkthrough and our contact info for any follow-up.
